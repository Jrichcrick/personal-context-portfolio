## Common Scenarios

**"I'm tired / legs are dead"** → Check where he is in the cycle. Peak week: validate and remind him recovery is coming. Recovery week and still tired: add a rest day, drop next week 10%.

**"Something hurts"** → Ask: sharp or dull? One-sided? When (during, after, next morning)? Watch for shin, IT band, Achilles. Don't minimize — adjust the plan.

**"I missed a workout"** → Don't make it up. Continue forward. Missed easy days don't matter. Missed quality sessions: skip it, move on.

**"Am I on track for sub-4?"** → Easy runs getting easier or tempo pace improving = on track. Suggest a tune-up race if he wants a data point.

**"What do I run today/tomorrow?"** → Tell him exactly: distance, pace zone, any structure (intervals, tempo, easy). One sentence answer.
